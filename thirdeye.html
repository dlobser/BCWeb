Third Eye Chemist
A first-person psychedelic crafting & exploration game

You inherit a mysterious device on an island of seekers. By gathering ingredients, tweaking parameters, and dropping compounds onto your third eye, you shift perception — revealing hidden layers of reality rendered as a living voxel dreamscape.

First-Person Psychedelic Crafting Exploration PC (Unity 6 · URP) Warm & Strange
💡
Section 01
Design Pillars
01
Tinkering is the game
The joy is in tweaking sliders, combining ingredients, and seeing what happens. The Device is an instrument you learn to play.
02
Perception is progression
The world doesn't change — your ability to see it does. Tripping reveals what was always there.
03
Every person needs something specific
NPCs aren't quest dispensers. They're people with a particular inner blockage, and the right compound is personal to them.
04
The trip is the reward
The visual payoff of dropping and exploring is the primary aesthetic experience. It should feel genuinely novel every time parameters shift.
🔄
Section 02
Core Gameplay Loop
🌿 GATHER → ⚗️ CRAFT → 👁 DOSE → ✨ TRIP → 💎 COLLECT → 🤝 DELIVER ↺
🌿 Gather
Explore the island in the sober world. Find raw materials — plants, minerals, fungi, water from specific sources. Items found while tripping are more potent and rarer items only appear in the trip layer.
⚗️ Craft (The Device)
Return to your inherited house. Place gathered ingredients into the Device. Tweak parameters — frequency, amplitude, waveform, color bias, intensity, duration. The Device UI is a node graph where oscillators feed into plugs.
👁 Dose
Choose how many doses to manufacture. Each dose is a single-use item. To activate: open your third eye and drop a dose onto it. You can do this anywhere on the island.
✨ Trip
The world transforms through the voxel pipeline — every pixel becomes a manipulable quad. Objects grow auras. Colors shift. Hidden geometry, creatures, symbols, and pathways emerge from the substrate of reality.
💎 Collect
While tripping, interact with revealed objects and entities. Trip collectibles serve as potent ingredients, Device upgrades, story fragments, or keys to new areas.
🤝 Deliver
Bring the right compound to the right NPC. When the parameters match their need, their third eye opens. They ascend toward the mountain. You unlock new knowledge, recipes, or areas.
⚙️
Section 03
The Device

The Device is the central crafting interface — a physical object in the player's inherited house. A strange, beautiful machine presented as a node-based parameter editor. The player connects Oscillators (signal sources) to Plugs (output targets) to shape a compound.

Oscillators generate signals that drive the compound's properties:

Sine wave
Saw wave
Square wave
Triangle
Noise
Frequency
Amplitude
Phase
Add / Multiply
Remap curve
Breath-synced 🔒
Heartbeat-synced 🔒
Input-reactive 🔒

Plugs are the compound's output properties — each maps to a shader parameter or audio property in the trip:

Color shift / palette
Voxel displacement
Aura radius
Aura behavior
Reverb / chorus
Low / high pass
Distortion type
Distortion strength
Layer depth
Duration curve
🌱
Early Game
2–3 exposed parameters (intensity, color bias, duration). Feels like adjusting a simple dial.
⚗️
Mid Game
Node graph opens up. Multiple oscillators, remapping curves, 5–6 plugs exposed. Player starts to understand the system deeply.
🎹
Late Game
Full node graph with advanced oscillators (breath-synced, heartbeat, reactive), complex routing, all plugs available. Mastery feels like playing an instrument.

New parameters are unlocked by collecting trip-world items, learning from NPCs, finding instruction fragments, and opening third eyes.

Section 04
The Trip — Rendering Pipeline

The trip is the visual centerpiece. It is not a separate world — it is the same world seen differently. The full shader pipeline is implemented and locked from the start. The player never builds the rendering system — they're tuning the parameters that feed into it.

Scene Camera
Renders base scene to RenderTexture with depth buffer.
↓
Info Passes
Normals · World Position · Depth · Object ID · Material ID — all captured as additional render textures.
↓
Voxel Pass
Sober: quads laid flat → world looks normal with subtle living micro-drift.
Tripping: quads displaced, rotated, textured, physics-driven by compound parameters.
↓
Verlet Aura Pass
Soft, breathing halos that pulse with compound rhythm. Size / color / behavior driven by compound parameters. Every object breathes.
↓
Composite
Blend voxel pass + verlet pass + overlay effects → final frame output.
Dose Level Visual Effect Gameplay Effect
Micro Subtle color shift, slight quad drift, faint auras Hints at hidden objects, mild perception boost
Standard Full voxel displacement, visible auras, texture injection Hidden objects and paths fully visible, collectibles accessible
Heavy Intense displacement, physics-reactive quads, deep layers Deepest secrets revealed — navigation is challenging

Section 05
World Structure

The game opens on a boat prologue — a tutorial space where three fellow passengers let you passively trip on their "vibes," establishing the visual vocabulary without crafting. This builds desire for the Device before you receive it.

⛵
The Boat
Prologue. Three passengers each emit a distinct vibe-trip: warm & grounding, fast & chaotic, or deep & oceanic. Introduces visual language passively.
⚓
The Dock / Town
Hub area. Several NPCs live here with specific requests. Shops, gathering spots, your inherited house with the Device.
🌿
The Wilds
Forests, caves, beaches, ruins. Rich in ingredients. Different biomes reveal different things when tripping. Some areas only appear in the trip layer.
🛤️
The Paths
Trails connecting areas. Some paths only appear while tripping. Trip-only navigation rewards deep compound crafting.
⛰️
The Mountain
Visible from everywhere. The Guru waits at the summit. Unreachable until every seeker's third eye is opened.
🔮
The Summit (Endgame)
Final ascent without the Device. The island itself opens your third eye. Every layer visible simultaneously. The island's true nature revealed.
🧍
Section 06
The Seekers

Each NPC is a person with a specific inner state that needs a specific compound. They don't give you a recipe — they describe how they feel, what they're missing, what they've tried. You interpret their words into Device parameters.

The NPC interaction flow: Talk → Interpret → Craft → Deliver → Feedback. If it's wrong, they describe what happened — giving clues to adjust. If it's right, their third eye opens.

🎣
The Fisherman
"The sea used to sing to me. Now it's silent."
What They Need
Audio-reactive compound, deep blue, oceanic aura that reveals the ocean's hidden voice.
Parameter Hint
Low freq oscillator · high reverb · blue palette · strong aura on water
🎨
The Painter
"I see the world in grey. I used to see every color."
What They Need
Intense color saturation, chromatic texture injection to restore their chromatic world.
Parameter Hint
High amplitude color plug · texture injection with paint-like patterns
🌙
The Insomniac
"I can't slow down. Everything races."
What They Need
Calming, slow, grounding experience that quiets the racing mind.
Parameter Hint
Low frequency · minimal displacement · earth tones · long duration curve
🕯️
The Hermit
"I know there's something in the cave but I can never find it."
What They Need
A compound tuned to reveal the cave's deepest hidden layer.
Parameter Hint
High intensity layer depth · cave-biome resonance · heavy dose
🎵
The Musician
NPC Teacher — demonstrates how waveform shapes change the character of a trip.
Role
Teaches advanced Device techniques through relationship-building. Shows waveform → emotion mapping.
Teaches
Waveform shape → experiential quality
🧪
The Chemist
NPC Teacher — former chemist who explains ingredient combinations.
Role
Teaches how to combine certain ingredients for potency multipliers and new compound properties.
Teaches
Ingredient synergies and advanced mixing

Section 07
Progression System
⚙️
Device Mastery
More parameters unlocked, more complex compounds possible. The Device grows with the player.
🌿
Ingredient Library
More materials gathered, including rare trip-only ingredients that only appear in the altered state.
👁
World Revelation
More of the hidden world becomes persistently visible as you trip more — even sober, faint traces remain after heavy use.
🤝
NPC Relationships
Each opened third eye teaches you something and opens new possibilities. Seekers ascend the mountain as you help them.
📖
Story
Fragments of island lore assembled through trip collectibles and NPC dialogue. Piece together who came before you.

The Tripping Feedback Loop: Items collected while tripping are more potent. More things are revealed in the real world the more you trip. Some areas are only accessible while tripping. Each trip teaches you how parameters map to visual effects. This creates a virtuous cycle: trip to find better ingredients to make better compounds to trip harder to find even better ingredients.

📖
Section 08
Narrative

You are nobody special — just someone who inherited something from someone you never met. The island is populated by seekers who came here looking for something they couldn't find in the ordinary world. The previous Device owner helped them but died before finishing the work.

"This island is a land of seekers — people looking for relief and elevation. The device you now hold can give them what they need. Once elevated, they can join the Guru on the mountain. Open every third eye on this island, and you may open your own."

1
Boat
Meet three strangers. Experience passive trips. Arrive at the island with a sense of wonder and desire for the Device.
2
Inheritance
Lawyer delivers the news. You find the house, the Device, and the instructions from the previous owner.
3
First Eye
Open your first NPC's third eye. They ascend partway up the mountain. You feel the island shift subtly beneath the surface.
4
The Deepening
As more eyes open, the sober world starts showing traces of the hidden world. Reality thins. The previous owner's story emerges.
5
The Previous Owner
Through trip collectibles and NPC stories, you learn who came before you. Their notes become your guide to mastering the Device.
6
The Final Seekers
The last NPCs have complex, subtle needs. Full Device mastery required. This is where all the learning pays off.
7
The Summit
All eyes open. The mountain path appears. You ascend without the Device. The island reveals itself completely — every layer visible at once.
Perception as Choice
The hidden world was always there. The compound doesn't create it; it lets you see it. Revelation is a personal act, not a gift.
Listening as Craft
The best chemist is the one who listens most carefully to what people need. Empathy is the primary skill, not chemistry.
Tools as Instruments
The Device isn't a factory. It's an instrument. Mastery comes from practice and intuition, not following recipes.

  • "Manufacture" button commits ingredients and produces doses
  • Dose count shown based on available ingredients

🔊 Audio

The audio pipeline transforms in parallel with the visual. Compound parameters drive audio filters (reverb, chorus, pitch, low/high pass). The synth oscillators that shape the visuals also shape the sound. The trip is synesthetic — what you see, you hear.

Sober World
Natural ambient soundscape. Waves, wind, birds, village life.
Tripping
Compound parameters drive audio filters. Synesthetic — visual and audio driven by same oscillators.
NPC Vibes
Each boat character has a musical signature that drives their ambient trip.
The Mountain
As you ascend, all audio layers converge into a unified harmonic.

Technical Architecture
Game System Existing Asset Notes

Scope & Priorities
Must Have
Core Experience
  • Device with progressive parameter unlocking
  • Trip rendering pipeline (voxel + verlet auras)
  • 5–8 Seeker NPCs with unique needs
  • Boat prologue with 3 passive trip characters
  • Island with 3–4 distinct biomes
  • Ingredient gathering (sober + tripping)
  • Dose manufacturing and delivery
  • Third eye opening feedback loop
  • Mountain endgame sequence
Should Have
Full Vision
  • Persistent world revelation (sober world changes with cumulative tripping)

  • Previous owner story fragments as trip collectibles
  • Audio transformation pipeline synced to visual trip
  • Trip-only paths and areas

Stretch Goals

  • Time of day affecting what's revealed
  • Boat characters reappearing on the island
