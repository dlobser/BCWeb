Brain Candy on the Road!

Events

Taking brain candy to conferences and festivals around the world

PS25

Denver, Colorado

The premier conference for psychedelic research, bringing together scientists, clinicians, and advocates. This event showcases the latest research in psychedelic medicine and consciousness studies.

I was an interloper this year as Deep Space was gone. Two years ago we shared Visitations with over 300 guests. This year we attended a half dozen afterparties with an audience that definitely 'got it'

Mind XR

Berlin, Germany

A conference focused on extended reality (XR) technologies and their applications in mental health and consciousness research. Mind XR explores the intersection of immersive technology and psychological well-being.

I was not able to attend this small conference in Berlin, but my science advisor, Till, was there sharing our poster.

AWE (Augmented World Expo)

Long Beach, California

The world's leading AR/VR conference and expo, featuring the latest in augmented and virtual reality technology. AWE brings together industry leaders, developers, and innovators in immersive technologies.

I gave a talk on flicker light stimulation in VR, and got in a little plug for Brain Candy as well, a video will be coming soon!

Creative Tech Week

Brooklyn, New York

A week-long celebration of creative technology, featuring workshops, exhibitions, and talks. This event showcases the intersection of art, technology, and innovation in Brooklyn's creative scene.

200 people signed up for one of hundreds of tiny events during this fascinating week. I was able to take over the big LED wall at NYU's Tandon. Big screens are a great medium for Brain Candy!

QRI Meetup

Amsterdam, Netherlands

A gathering organized by the Qualia Research Institute, focused on consciousness research and the scientific study of subjective experience. This meetup brings together researchers and enthusiasts exploring the nature of consciousness.

My first immersion in this amazing community.

ETH Denver

Denver, Colorado

One of the largest Ethereum and blockchain conferences in the world, featuring developers, entrepreneurs, and blockchain enthusiasts. ETH Denver showcases the latest innovations in decentralized technology and Web3.

Brain Candy was part of a collection of experiences shown on a pop up dome and presented by the Cyberdelic Nexus.

IVRHA (International VR Healthcare Association)

Sarasota, Florida

A conference dedicated to the application of virtual reality in healthcare and medical training. IVRHA brings together healthcare professionals, researchers, and VR developers to explore therapeutic applications of immersive technology.

Bob Fine was kind enough to invite us down to present our poster about Brain Candy.
