Creative Technology Studio

Studio Mission

light.clinic creates software, hardware, and installations for the wellness and mindfulness spaces. We are seeking funds to pursue R&D and full production of a series of apps, devices, and experiences in support of therapeutic altered states.

Our team has a wide range of experience creating art & science for therapeutic, mind-altering experiences across multiple platforms and mediums.

Hardware Innovation

🔄 "Day Tripper" Recreation

Recreate the LSD "Day Tripper", a low tech, mechanical, breath driven flicker light to use with the sun or any bright light source. Very popular at Grateful Dead shows.

👓 Low Cost Strobe Goggles

Affordable entry point for therapeutic flicker light experiences, making the technology accessible to a broader audience.

🫶 "Heavy Pillow" Device

You hold on your chest, as you breathe, it synchronizes vibrations to create a purring cat effect for therapeutic relaxation.

🌊 Tactile Relaxation Blanket

Heavy blanket with rows of actuators designed to create waves of vibratory relaxation for enhanced wellness experiences.
