Description

Brain Candy is a VR experience that uses flickering lights and visual patterns to guide your brain into various states of consciousness. Drawing on both scientific research and ancient practices, it offers a collection of audio-visual experiences designed to promote relaxation, focus, and creative states of mind.

Short Description (50 words)

Brain Candy uses strobing lights and dynamic visuals in VR to alter your brain state, helping you relax, focus, or energize based on scientific research into neural resonance and brainwave entrainment.

Medium Description (100 words)

Brain Candy is a VR experience that uses flickering lights and visual patterns to guide your brain into altered states. Based on research into neural rhythms and sensory processing, each "candy" represents a unique combination of audio-visual stimulation designed to promote specific mental states - from deep relaxation to heightened focus and creativity. The app combines scientific principles with artistic expression to create an immersive journey into consciousness exploration.

Full Description

Brain Candy uses flickering lights and visual patterns guided by research into altered states by Heinrich Kluver to guide your brain into deep states of relaxation, focus, and energy. We bring science, art, and fun together for an unforgettable experience.

The flickering light enters your body through your open eyes, reaching the central brain and sparking neural rhythms and hyperconnectivity which can help you sync, energize, and breathe your way into a harmonious state.

Whether you're looking to melt away stress, boost your focus, or unlock your creativity, Brain Candy is here to sweeten your day. Our intuitive design makes it easy for anyone to explore and enjoy.

From calming relaxation to bursts of mental energy, Brain Candy helps you discover elusive, ephemeral, altered states. Let our light pulses guide you to a place of wonder, awe, and inner balance.

Not Recommended For:

  • Those uncomfortable with intense visual experiences
  • People seeking traditional gaming experiences with objectives and challenges
  • Users who experience significant VR motion sickness
Important Safety Notice

Brain Candy features strobing visuals that may not be suitable for individuals with photosensitive epilepsy or similar conditions. Please consult the warnings page for full details.
