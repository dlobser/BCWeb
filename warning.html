Photosensitivity and Seizure Warning

Brain Candy contains intense strobing lights, flashing patterns, and visual effects that may trigger seizures or other adverse reactions in photosensitive individuals.

Who Is At Risk?

You should NOT use Brain Candy if you:

  • Have a history of epilepsy or seizures
  • Are sensitive to flashing lights or patterns
  • Have been diagnosed with photosensitive epilepsy
  • Have any form of neurological condition
  • Are pregnant
  • Are under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Are experiencing fatigue or sleep deprivation

Warning Signs and Symptoms

Stop using Brain Candy immediately and seek medical attention if you experience any of these symptoms:

  • Lightheadedness or dizziness
  • Altered vision or visual disturbances
  • Eye or muscle twitching
  • Confusion or disorientation
  • Nausea
  • Loss of awareness
  • Involuntary movements
  • Convulsions

Safety Precautions

  • Use Brain Candy when seated or laying down
  • Stop immediately if you feel uncomfortable
  • Do not use when tired or sleep-deprived
  • Consider having someone nearby during first use

By using Brain Candy, you acknowledge that you have read and understood these warnings and accept all associated risks.
