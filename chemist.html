🎲 How to Play
Each day: Roll dice to automate your session, or manually navigate each tab.

Stats: Education (🎓) boosts synthesis. Charisma (✨) builds friendships. Delusion (🌀) rises if you trip without integrating — it limits options and can destabilize friends.

Synthesis: You need knowledge cards + equipment + chemicals. Without knowledge, you're guessing. With it, slots are pre-labeled and purity is higher.

Friends: Each has their own stats. They can supply materials, join trips, and share knowledge. Neglect or exploit them and watch their Delusion rise.

Integration: Spend tokens after every trip. Skip and face consequences. The Shulgin path requires impeccable integration discipline.

Career tracks: Party promoter → dealer → pharma exec → DEA-licensed researcher. Or: pure psychonaut → consciousness researcher. Your stats unlock different paths.
