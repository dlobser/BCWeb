About Brain Candy

Discover the science and inspiration behind the sweet journey of Brain Candy.

The Science of Light

Brain Candy uses flickering lights and visual patterns guided by research into altered states by Heinrich Kluver to guide your brain into deep states of relaxation, focus, and energy. We bring science, art, and fun together for an unforgettable experience.

How It Works

The flickering light enters your body through your open eyes, reaching the central brain and sparking neural rhythms and hyperconnectivity. The elusive, metaphorical 'third eye' (the pineal gland) and thalamus work together to help you sync, energize, and breathe your way into a harmonious state.

Designed for Everyone

Whether you're looking to melt away stress, boost your focus, or unlock your creativity, Brain Candy is here to sweeten your day. Our intuitive design makes it easy for anyone to explore and enjoy.

Sweet States of Mind

From calming relaxation to bursts of mental energy, Brain Candy helps you discover elusive, ephemeral, altered states. Let our light pulses guide you to a place of wonder, awe, and inner balance.
